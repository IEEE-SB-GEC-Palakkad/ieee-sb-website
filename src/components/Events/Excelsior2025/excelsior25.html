What is Excelsior?

A three-day celebration of technology, creativity, and community. We blend workshops, competitions, and performances in a space designed to feel beautifully in-between—soft edges, floating light, and room to dream.

Workshops

Hands-on labs that glow with curiosity and collaboration.

Performances

Stages that shimmer—music, art, and stories under neon haze.

Build Nights

Late nights, bright minds, and that soft hum of becoming.

Multiple technical workshops on trending domains (AI/ML,LLM,Cyber security,IOT, etc)

Expert talk sessions and panel discussions with industriak professionals and researchers

Networking oppurtunity with students and faculties from multiple colleges.

Cultural interactions and community-building sessions.

FAQ
Who all can participate in the event ?
There is no specific criteria for participation. Anyone interested can participate in the event.
Should I register for each event separately ?
It is your preference that matters. You can join for more than an event in Excelsior’25 provided you abide by the terms and conditions. For the same reason we have provided packages which is a mixture of events. You can choose package according to your taste and preference.
Will I be refunded if I decide to cancel my registration ?
Refund policies depend on the terms and conditions of the event. Please refer to the official refund guidelines before registering.
Is food and accomodation available?

ABOUT IEEE SB GEC PKD

The IEEE Students branch of Government Engineering College Palakkad, works for providing an ideal platform for young minds to nourish their technical skills as an engineer by providing a technical home. The SB comprises actively working with IAS, ComSoc, and Computer Society and also the WiE affinity group in association for achieving the objectives.
